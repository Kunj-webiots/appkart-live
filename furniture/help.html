Help & Customer Care Content

If you have questions about your order, need support with a return, or just want help choosing the right furniture for your space, you’re in the right place. We’ve gathered answers to some of the most common customer questions to save you time — but if you don’t find what you’re looking for, our support team is just a message away.

  • 🛒 Order & Delivery

    Track your order, update your address, and check estimated delivery times. We’ll notify you with real-time updates once your order is dispatched.

  • 💳 Payments & Refunds

    Learn about payment methods, refund timelines, and what to do if a transaction fails. Refunds are processed within 3–7 business days once approved.

  • 🔄 Returns & Cancellations

    Changed your mind? You can cancel your order before it’s shipped. For returns, ensure the item is unused and reported within the return window.

  • 🔐 Account & Security

    Update your account details, manage your password, and keep your personal data secure. Contact us if you notice any suspicious activity.

  • 🛠 Installation Help

    For items that need setup, our service team will contact you post-delivery to schedule installation at your convenience.
